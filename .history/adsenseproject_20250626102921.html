Oil Palm Production and Establishment

Oil palm {Elaesis guineensis} industry is one high profit yielding business in Nigeria, contributing greatly to the economy of the country, provide employment and foreign exchange commodity. The products from oil palm such as the crude oil, palm kernel oil are of high demand globally for food, cosmetics and other industrial raw material. The average lifespan of a palm tree is about 80 years and maintains profitable production throughout this period, this quality makes oil palm establishment a lifetime investment for investors. It takes 3-4 years from the time of transplanting to main field for oil palm to start producing fruits. The average yield per hectare per year is approximately 3-8 tons of crude oil. Key Factors for Palm Plantation Establishment

  • Soil Type: Well-drained, fertile soils are ideal.
  • Climate: Requires a humid tropical climate with adequate rainfall.
  • Spacing: Proper spacing between palms to ensure optimal growth.
  • Maintenance: Regular weeding, fertilization, and pest control.

Investing in oil palm production not only supports local economies but also contributes to sustainable agricultural practices.
